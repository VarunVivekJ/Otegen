The Company has installed high capacity, state – of- the art automatic plant & Machinery at its factory to carry on production of high quality ayurvedic products uninterruptedly. Mission

The company’s endeavor is to serve the humanity by producing best quality and efficacious Ayurvedic Medicines in capsule, liquid orals, ointment, gels, granules form through rigorous Research work. Its products comprise of ayurvedic formulations in prescription, OTC, Dietary Supplement category for almost all ailments of human being. Beginning with 30 products initially, Otegen Global has come a long way and now it has more than 150 products to its credit. Otegen Global has also launched more than 50 single herbs products as health & dietary supplements meant for export purpose. It also has a vision and aim to launch herbal products for the hotel industry and wellness centers in both domestic and overseas market in the same year.

Commitment

To popularize the 5000 year old system of medicine viz. Ayurveda effectively among the population for their benefit by preserving natural effects of rare herbs as per ancient texts & scriptures without altering basics but ENCAPSULATING.
